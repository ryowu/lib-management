Description and Contact

The Library Management System is an online system that allows users to borrow and return books, and staff to manage books using a CRUD window.

Our mission is to make it easier for people to access and manage books. With our system, users can search for books, check availability, and request to borrow or reserve them online. Staff can manage the book collection, including adding new books, updating book information, and managing borrowing requests.

Our system is easy to use and accessible from anywhere, so users can access the library services and manage their borrowing needs online.

Features of Library Management System

Borrow Books Online: Users can easily search for and borrow books online using our intuitive search interface.

Return Books Online: Returning books is just as easy as borrowing them. Users can mark books as returned with a single click.

CRUD Operations: Library staff can manage the collection of books with our easy-to-use Create, Read, Update, and Delete (CRUD) interface.

Real-Time Availability: Users can see in real-time whether a book is available or currently checked out by another user.

Automatic Reminders: Our system automatically sends reminders to users when their borrowed books are due for return, helping to reduce late returns.
